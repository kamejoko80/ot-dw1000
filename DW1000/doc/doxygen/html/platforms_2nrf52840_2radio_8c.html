Note

Buffer pointed by the p_data pointer may be modified by the function handler (and other modules) until nrf_drv_radio802154_buffer_free() function is called.
Parameters
[in]p_dataPointer to buffer containing received data. First byte in the buffer is length of the frame and following bytes is the frame itself (after PHR).
[in]powerRSSI of received frame.
[in]lqiLQI of received frame.
